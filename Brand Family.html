Global Brand Montage

Mondelēz International brings powerhouse brands that offer quality, convenience and truly delicious taste to consumers all over the world.

Cadbury Bournvita malted drink debuted in 1948 in India, the same year Cadbury India was established. In India, Cadbury Bournvita is one of India’s most trusted brands and comes in both a chocolate-and-caramel flavor called Cadbury Bournvita 5 Star Magic. In Nigeria, Cadbury Bournvita comes in a 500g size and it’s known as “the food drink for vitality” and is endorsed by the Nutrition Society of Nigeria. Markets: India, Nigeria
Cadbury was founded in 1824 when John Cadbury opened a grocer’s shop at 93 Bull Street, Birmingham, England. Among other things, he sold cocoa and drinking chocolate, which he prepared himself using a pestle and mortar. Now, the brand offers a host of delicious chocolates and drinks, including the Cadbury Egg, Cadbury Dairy Milk chocolates, Flake, Wispa, Twirl and Eclairs chocolates, and Cadbury Bourneville drinking chocolate. Related Links: Cadbury UK, Cadbury Australia, Cadbury Kitchen (Australia), Cadbury NZ, Cadbury Fact Sheet Markets: Australia, Canada, Malaysia, New Zealand, Nigeria, South Africa, United Kingdom
Cadbury Buttermint confectionery is a two-in-one sweet that gives consumers the benefits of enjoying the creamy taste of butter, perfectly blended with mint, for a fresh taste. With Cadbury Buttermint, taste and refreshment go hand in hand. Market: Nigeria
Cadbury Hot Chocolate Drink 3in1 is a delicious tasting, rich and creamy Hot Chocolate Drink that is best in class and produced by Cadbury. It contains the finest cocoa and an optimum blend of milk and sugar. It’s a complete hot chocolate drink on its own and does not require any additives to be enjoyed. Market: Nigeria
Clorets is a refreshing gum and mint which gives a long-lasting refreshing taste designed to neutralize food, drink and cigarette odors by using an element called chlorophyll. It is the only chewing gum that contains the exclusive ingredient Actizol - so you can be closer to anyone you want, anywhere you want, anytime you want. Did you know that Clorets gum's refreshing effect in Japan is known as "sukkiri"? This word is now an important part of Clorets communication in that country! Markets: Brazil, Japan, Malaysia, Nigeria, Thailand
TomTom, our flagship candy in Nigeria, is a big elliptical, black and white sweet, with strong menthol flavour. TomTom is the first Nigerian candy and it has been around since 1970. TomTom delivers quality, fresh breath and instant sweet relief and is available everywhere in Nigeria. That’s the TomTom promise and that’s what the brand consistently delivers to our consumers. Market: Nigeria
